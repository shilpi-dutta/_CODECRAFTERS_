Explore Johar Jharkhand

Discover the rich culture, tribal heritage, waterfalls, hills, and national parks of Jharkhand. Experience nature and traditions like never before.

Netarhat

The Queen of Chotanagpur, known for sunrise and sunset views amidst lush forests.

Betla National Park

A wildlife paradise with elephants, tigers, and rich biodiversity.

Hundru Falls

One of the highest waterfalls in India, cascading from 320 feet.

Patratu Valley

Scenic drive with winding roads, green hills, and the beautiful Patratu Dam.

Deoghar (Baidyanath Dham)

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas, a major pilgrimage site for devotees.

Dassam Falls

Gushing waterfalls surrounded by dense forests, perfect for adventure lovers.
